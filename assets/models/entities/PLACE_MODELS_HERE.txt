Drop entity models here as .glb, named exactly:

  smiler.glb    (Level 0  — The Smiler)
  hound.glb     (Level 1  — The Hound)
  crawler.glb   (Level 2  — The Crawler)
  drowned.glb   (Level 37 — The Drowned)

Missing files fall back to the built-in procedural creatures.
See ../README.md for the Blender export conventions.
